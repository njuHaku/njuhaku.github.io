Grassmann 数

Grassmann 数的基本性质是：任意两个 Grassmann 数 θ,η\theta,\etaθ,η 之间遵循反对易关系：

其中 A,BA,BA,B 为对易数。

(∏i∫dθi∗dθi)e−θi∗Bijθj=(∏i∫dθi∗dθi)e−∑ibiθi∗θi=∏ibi=det⁡B(21)\begin{aligned} (\prod_i\int d\theta^*_i d\theta_i )e^{-\theta_i^* B_{ij}\theta_j} &= (\prod_i\int d\theta^*_i d\theta_i )e^{-\sum_{i}b_i\theta_i^* \theta_i}\\ &= \prod_{i}b_i = \det B\\ \end{aligned}\tag{21} (i∏​∫dθi∗​dθi​)e−θi∗​Bij​θj​​=(i∏​∫dθi∗​dθi​)e−∑i​bi​θi∗​θi​=i∏​bi​=detB​(21)

用矩阵形式写出更简洁：

∫dθ†dθe−θ†Bθ=det⁡B\int d\bm{\theta}^\dagger d\bm{\theta} e^{-\bm{\theta}^\dagger\bm{B}\bm{\theta}} = \det \bm{B} ∫dθ†dθe−θ†Bθ=detB

以及：

(∏i∫dθi∗dθi)θkθl∗e−θi∗Bijθj=(det⁡B)(B−1)kl(22)(\prod_i\int d\theta^*_i d\theta_i )\theta_k\theta_l^* e^{-\theta_i^* B_{ij}\theta_j} = (\det B)(B^{-1})_{kl}\tag{22} (i∏​∫dθi∗​dθi​)θk​θl∗​e−θi∗​Bij​θj​=(detB)(B−1)kl​(22)

用矩阵形式给出证明：
具体来说，先对上式左边进行幺正变换：

θ=Uθ′θ†=θ′†U†\bm{\theta} = \bm{U}\bm{\theta}'\quad \bm{\theta}^\dagger =\bm{\theta}'^\dagger \bm{U}^\dagger θ=Uθ′θ†=θ′†U†

使得 B\bm{B}B 对角化：

U†BU=b=diag(b1,⋯ ,bi,⋯ )\bm{U}^\dagger\bm{B}\bm{U} = \bm{b} = \mathrm{diag}(b_1,\cdots,b_i,\cdots) U†BU=b=diag(b1​,⋯,bi​,⋯)

由此可得：

B−1=Ub−1U†\bm{B}^{-1} = \bm{U}\bm{b}^{-1}\bm{U}^\dagger B−1=Ub−1U†

进行幺正变换后，(22)(22)(22) 式左边成为：

∫dθ†dθ(θθ†)kle−θ†Bθ=∫dθ′†dθ′(Uθ′θ′†U†)kle−θ′†bθ′=∫dθ′†dθ′(Ub−1U†)kle−θ′†bθ′=∫dθ′†dθ′(B−1)kle−θ′†bθ′=(det⁡B)(B−1)kl\begin{aligned} \int d\bm{\theta}^\dagger d\bm{\theta} (\bm{\theta\theta}^\dagger)_{kl}e^{-\bm{\theta}^\dagger\bm{B}\bm{\theta}} &= \int d\bm{\theta}'^\dagger d\bm{\theta}'(\bm{U}\bm{\theta}'\bm{\theta}'^\dagger \bm{U}^\dagger)_{kl}e^{-\bm{\theta}'^\dagger\bm{b}\bm{\theta}'}\\ &= \int d\bm{\theta}'^\dagger d\bm{\theta}'(\bm{U}\bm{b}^{-1}\bm{U}^\dagger)_{kl}e^{-\bm{\theta}'^\dagger\bm{b}\bm{\theta}'}\\ &= \int d\bm{\theta}'^\dagger d\bm{\theta}'(\bm{B}^{-1})_{kl}e^{-\bm{\theta}'^\dagger\bm{b}\bm{\theta}'}\\ &= (\det \bm{B})(\bm{B}^{-1})_{kl}\\ \end{aligned} ∫dθ†dθ(θθ†)kl​e−θ†Bθ​=∫dθ′†dθ′(Uθ′θ′†U†)kl​e−θ′†bθ′=∫dθ′†dθ′(Ub−1U†)kl​e−θ′†bθ′=∫dθ′†dθ′(B−1)kl​e−θ′†bθ′=(detB)(B−1)kl​​

即与(22)(22)(22) 式右边形式一致，得证。上式中第三步运用了 Grassmann 数积分的性质，不妨类比 (19)(19)(19) 中第二式。

Dirac 场的量子化

有了关于 Grassmann 数的基础。我们现在可以对旋量场进行量子化了。现在我们可以将一个费米子场 ψ(x)\psi(x)ψ(x) 表示为：

ψ(x)=∑iψiϕi(x)\psi(x) = \sum_i \psi_i\phi_i(x) ψ(x)=i∑​ψi​ϕi​(x)

其中 ϕi(x)\phi_i(x)ϕi​(x) 为 c-number 的函数。ψi\psi_iψi​ 为 Grassmann 数。为了描述 Dirac 场，我们取 ϕi\phi_iϕi​ 为四分量旋量的基底。

Dirac 场的两点关联函数为：

⟨0∣Tψ(x1)ψˉ(x2)∣0⟩=∫DψˉDψexp⁡[i∫d4xψˉ(i∂−m)ψ]ψ(x1)ψˉ(x2)∫DψˉDψexp⁡[i∫d4xψˉ(i∂−m)ψ](23)\begin{aligned} \langle 0|T\psi(x_1)\bar{\psi}(x_2)|0\rangle &= \frac{\int \mathcal{D}\bar{\psi}\mathcal{D}\psi \exp[i\int d^4x \bar{\psi}(i\cancel{\partial}-m)\psi]\psi(x_1)\bar{\psi}(x_2)}{\int \mathcal{D}\bar{\psi}\mathcal{D}\psi \exp[i\int d^4x \bar{\psi}(i\cancel{\partial}-m)\psi] } \end{aligned}\tag{23} ⟨0∣Tψ(x1​)ψˉ​(x2​)∣0⟩​=∫Dψˉ​Dψexp[i∫d4xψˉ​(i∂​−m)ψ]∫Dψˉ​Dψexp[i∫d4xψˉ​(i∂​−m)ψ]ψ(x1​)ψˉ​(x2​)​​(23)

其结果为：

⟨0∣Tψ(x1)ψˉ(x2)∣0⟩=det⁡(i∂−m)∫d4k(2π)4(−i(i∂−m))−1e−ik⋅(x1−x2)det⁡(i∂−m)=∫d4k(2π)4ie−ik⋅(x1−x2)k−m+iϵ=SF(x1−x2)(24)\begin{aligned} \langle 0|T\psi(x_1)\bar{\psi}(x_2)|0\rangle &= \frac{\det(i\cancel{\partial}-m)\int \frac{d^4k}{(2\pi)^4}(-i(i\cancel{\partial}-m))^{-1}e^{-ik\cdot(x_1-x_2)}}{\det(i\cancel{\partial}-m)}\\ &= \int \frac{d^4k}{(2\pi)^4}\frac{ie^{-ik\cdot(x_1-x_2)}}{\cancel{k}-m + i\epsilon} = S_F(x_1-x_2)\\ \end{aligned}\tag{24} ⟨0∣Tψ(x1​)ψˉ​(x2​)∣0⟩​=det(i∂​−m)det(i∂​−m)∫(2π)4d4k​(−i(i∂​−m))−1e−ik⋅(x1​−x2​)​=∫(2π)4d4k​k​−m+iϵie−ik⋅(x1​−x2​)​=SF​(x1​−x2​)​(24)

Dirac 场的生成泛函为：

Z[ηˉ,η]=∫DψDψˉexp⁡[i∫d4x[ψˉ(i∂−m)ψ+ηˉψ+ψˉη]](25)Z[\bar{\eta},\eta] = \int \mathcal{D}\psi\mathcal{D}\bar{\psi}\exp[i\int d^4x[\bar{\psi}(i\cancel{\partial}-m)\psi + \bar{\eta}\psi + \bar{\psi}\eta]]\tag{25} Z[ηˉ​,η]=∫DψDψˉ​exp[i∫d4x[ψˉ​(i∂​−m)ψ+ηˉ​ψ+ψˉ​η]](25)

其中 η\etaη 是一个 Grassmann 场。

考虑到 SF(x−y)S_F(x-y)SF​(x−y) 是 Dirac 方程的格林函数：

(i∂−m)SF(x−y)=iδ(4)(x−y)(26)(i\cancel{\partial}-m)S_F(x-y) = i\delta^{(4)}(x-y)\tag{26} (i∂​−m)SF​(x−y)=iδ(4)(x−y)(26)

因此，可以考虑对 ψ,ψˉ\psi,\bar{\psi}ψ,ψˉ​ 进行如下 shift：

ψ=ψ′−i∫d4ySF(x−y)η(y)ψˉ=ψˉ′+i∫d4ySF∗(x−y)ηˉ(y)=ψˉ′−i∫d4ySF(y−x)ηˉ(y)\begin{aligned} \psi &= \psi' - i\int d^4y S_F(x-y)\eta(y)\\ \bar{\psi} &= \bar{\psi}' + i\int d^4y S_F^*(x-y)\bar{\eta}(y)\\ &= \bar{\psi}' - i\int d^4y S_F(y-x)\bar{\eta}(y)\\ \end{aligned} ψψˉ​​=ψ′−i∫d4ySF​(x−y)η(y)=ψˉ​′+i∫d4ySF∗​(x−y)ηˉ​(y)=ψˉ​′−i∫d4ySF​(y−x)ηˉ​(y)​

代入 (25)(25)(25) 式，得到：

Z[ηˉ,η]=∫DψDψˉexp⁡[i∫d4x[ψˉ(i∂−m)ψ+ηˉψ+ψˉη]]=∫Dψ′Dψˉ′exp⁡[i∫d4x[ψˉ′(i∂−m)ψ′]exp⁡[−∫d4xd4yη(x)SF(x−y)η(y)]=Z0exp⁡[−∫d4xd4yηˉ(x)SF(x−y)η(y)]\begin{aligned} Z[\bar{\eta},\eta] &= \int \mathcal{D}\psi\mathcal{D}\bar{\psi}\exp[i\int d^4x[\bar{\psi}(i\cancel{\partial}-m)\psi + \bar{\eta}\psi + \bar{\psi}\eta]]\\ &= \int \mathcal{D}\psi'\mathcal{D}\bar{\psi}'\exp[i\int d^4x [\bar{\psi}'(i\cancel{\partial}-m)\psi']\exp[-\int d^4xd^4y\eta(x)S_F(x-y)\eta(y)]\\ &=Z_0\exp[-\int d^4xd^4y\bar{\eta}(x)S_F(x-y)\eta(y)]\\ \end{aligned} Z[ηˉ​,η]​=∫DψDψˉ​exp[i∫d4x[ψˉ​(i∂​−m)ψ+ηˉ​ψ+ψˉ​η]]=∫Dψ′Dψˉ​′exp[i∫d4x[ψˉ​′(i∂​−m)ψ′]exp[−∫d4xd4yη(x)SF​(x−y)η(y)]=Z0​exp[−∫d4xd4yηˉ​(x)SF​(x−y)η(y)]​

即：

Z[ηˉ,η]=Z0exp⁡[−∫d4xd4yηˉ(x)SF(x−y)η(y)](27)Z[\bar{\eta},\eta] = Z_0\exp[-\int d^4xd^4y\bar{\eta}(x)S_F(x-y)\eta(y)] \tag{27} Z[ηˉ​,η]=Z0​exp[−∫d4xd4yηˉ​(x)SF​(x−y)η(y)](27)

有关 Grassmann 数的求导，有：

ddηθη=−θddηηθ=θ\frac{d}{d\eta}\theta\eta = -\theta \quad \frac{d}{d\eta}\eta\theta = \theta dηd​θη=−θdηd​ηθ=θ

这样，我们可以用泛函导数与生成泛函写出两点关联函数：

⟨0∣Tψ(x1)ψˉ(x2)∣0⟩=Z0−1(−iδδηˉ(x1))(iδδη(x2))Z[ηˉ,η]∣ηˉ,η=0(28)\langle 0|T\psi(x_1)\bar{\psi}(x_2)|0\rangle = Z_0^{-1}(-i\frac{\delta}{\delta \bar{\eta}(x_1)})(i\frac{\delta}{\delta \eta(x_2)})Z[\bar{\eta},\eta]|_{\bar{\eta},\eta = 0} \tag{28} ⟨0∣Tψ(x1​)ψˉ​(x2​)∣0⟩=Z0−1​(−iδηˉ​(x1​)δ​)(iδη(x2​)δ​)Z[ηˉ​,η]∣ηˉ​,η=0​(28)

利用 (27)(27)(27) 式，立即得到上式的结果为：

⟨0∣Tψ(x1)ψˉ(x2)∣0⟩=SF(x1−x2)\langle 0|T\psi(x_1)\bar{\psi}(x_2)|0\rangle = S_F(x_1-x_2) ⟨0∣Tψ(x1​)ψˉ​(x2​)∣0⟩=SF​(x1​−x2​)

QED

量子场论的路径积分表述让我们可以直接从拉氏量出发，推导费曼规则。现在我们来看 QED 的拉氏量：

LQED=ψˉ(iD−m)ψ−14(Fμν)2=ψˉ(i∂−m)ψ−14(Fμν)2−eψˉγμψAμ=L0−eψˉγμψAμ\begin{aligned} \mathcal{L}_{QED} &= \bar{\psi}(i\cancel{D}-m)\psi - \frac{1}{4}(F_{\mu\nu})^2\\ &= \bar{\psi}(i\cancel{\partial}-m)\psi- \frac{1}{4}(F_{\mu\nu})^2 - e\bar{\psi}\gamma^{\mu}\psi A_{\mu}\\ &= \mathcal{L}_0 - e\bar{\psi}\gamma^{\mu}\psi A_{\mu}\\ \end{aligned} LQED​​=ψˉ​(iD​−m)ψ−41​(Fμν​)2=ψˉ​(i∂​−m)ψ−41​(Fμν​)2−eψˉ​γμψAμ​=L0​−eψˉ​γμψAμ​​

可以进行展开：

exp⁡[i∫L0][1−ie∫d4xψˉγμψAμ+⋯ ]\exp[i\int\mathcal{L}_0][1-ie\int d^4x \bar{\psi}\gamma^{\mu}\psi A_{\mu} + \cdots] exp[i∫L0​][1−ie∫d4xψˉ​γμψAμ​+⋯]

其中自由场部分对应如下传播子：

∫d4p(2π)4ie−ip⋅(x−y)p−m+iϵ\int \frac{d^4p}{(2\pi)^4}\frac{ie^{-ip\cdot(x-y)}}{\cancel{p}-m+i\epsilon} ∫(2π)4d4p​p​−m+iϵie−ip⋅(x−y)​

∫d4q(2π)4−igμνe−iq⋅(x−y)q2+iϵ\int \frac{d^4q}{(2\pi)^4}\frac{-ig_{\mu\nu}e^{-iq\cdot(x-y)}}{q^2+i\epsilon} ∫(2π)4d4q​q2+iϵ−igμν​e−iq⋅(x−y)​

相互作用项给出 QED 顶点：

−ieγμ∫d4x-ie\gamma^{\mu}\int d^4x −ieγμ∫d4x

以上就是坐标空间中的费曼规则，我们将其重排后可以得到动量空间中的费曼规则。

∫DψˉDψexp⁡[i∫d4xψˉ(iD−m)ψ]\int \mathcal{D}\bar{\psi}\mathcal{D}\psi \exp[i\int d^4x \bar{\psi}(i\cancel{D}-m)\psi] ∫Dψˉ​Dψexp[i∫d4xψˉ​(iD​−m)ψ]

det⁡(iD−m)=det⁡(i∂−m−eA)=det⁡(i∂−m)⋅det⁡(1−ii∂−m(−ieA))(28)\begin{aligned} \det(i\cancel{D}-m) &= \det(i\cancel{\partial} - m -e\cancel{A})\\ &= \det(i\cancel{\partial}-m)\cdot \det(1 - \frac{i}{i\cancel{\partial} - m}(-ie\cancel{A}))\\ \end{aligned}\tag{28} det(iD​−m)​=det(i∂​−m−eA​)=det(i∂​−m)⋅det(1−i∂​−mi​(−ieA​))​(28)

设 bib_ibi​ 为 BBB 的本征值，那么有下列公式成立：

det⁡B=∏ibi=exp⁡[∑ilog⁡bi]=exp⁡[Tr(log⁡B)]\det B = \prod_i b_i = \exp[\sum_i \log b_i] = \exp[\mathrm{Tr}(\log B)] detB=i∏​bi​=exp[i∑​logbi​]=exp[Tr(logB)]
